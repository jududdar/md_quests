# Soulbinder Elendalira



[Soulbinder Elendalira](/npc/61067) is a level 1 High Elf Warrior that spawns in [Northern Felwithe](/zone/61).

>**Soulbinder Elendalira says:** Greetings Soandso. When a hero of our world is slain their soul returns to the place it was last bound and the body is reincarnated. As a member of the Order of Eternity it is my duty to [bind your soul] to this location if that is your wish.

**You say:** `bind my soul`



>**Soulbinder Elendalira says:** Binding your soul. You will return here when you die.


**Soulbinder Elendalira casts:** [Bind Affinity](/spell/2049) on target.
