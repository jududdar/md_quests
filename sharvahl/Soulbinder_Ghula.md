# Soulbinder Ghula



[Soulbinder Ghula](/npc/155090) is a level 1 Vah Shir Warrior that spawns in [The City of Shar Vahl](/zone/155).





## Dialog

**You say:** `Hail`



>**Soulbinder Ghula says:** Greetings Soandso. When a hero of our world is slain their soul returns to the place it was last bound and the body is reincarnated. As a member of the Order of Eternity  it is my duty to [bind your soul] to this location if that is your wish.

**You say:** `bind my soul`



>**Soulbinder Ghula says:** Binding your soul. You will return here when you die.


**Soulbinder Ghula casts:** [Bind Affinity](/spell/2049) on target.
end
