# Exiled Officer of Marr
## On NPC Spawn

**Set a timer** named *depop* for 120 seconds

**Set a timer** named *fd* for 0 seconds

## Timer(s)

if(e.timer == "depop") then


**Exiled Officer of Marr despawns.**

elseif(e.timer == "fd") then


**Stop timer** named *fd*


e.self:SetAppearance(3); 
end
