# Soulbinder Gendal



[Soulbinder Gendal](/npc/77000) is a level 65 Skeleton Warrior that spawns in [The Arena](/zone/77).





## Dialog

**You say:** `hail`



>**Soulbinder Gendal says:** Greetings Soandso. When a hero of our world is slain their soul returns to the place it was last bound and the body is reincarnated. As a member of the Order of Eternity  it is my duty to [bind your soul] to this location if that is your wish.

**You say:** `bind my soul`



>**Soulbinder Gendal says:** Binding your soul. You will return here when you die.


**Soulbinder Gendal casts:** [Bind Affinity](/spell/2049) on target.
end
