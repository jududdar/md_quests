# Izibat



[Izibat](/npc/150152) is a level 50 Gnome Shopkeeper that spawns in [Shadow Haven](/zone/150).



## Dialog

**You say:** `hail`



if **Faction** >= Indifferent then



>**Izibat says:** Hail, Soandso! Welcome to Izibat's wondrous robes! I have many magnificent wares in stock, but don't delay, supplies are going fast!


else



>**Izibat says:** I am sorry but I am only allowed to make dealings with persons that have found favor in the Midst quarter. Please see Lamukas to inquire about how to further strengthen your alliance with the people of the Midst.

end
