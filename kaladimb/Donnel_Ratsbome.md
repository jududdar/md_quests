# Donnel Ratsbome



[Donnel Ratsbome](/npc/67085) is a level 41 Dwarf Banker that spawns in [North Kaladim](/zone/67).



## Dialog

**You say:** `armor`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** If armor it is you're seeking, then go no further than Everhot Forge in North Kaladim.  The Everhots are the finest smiths to have walked Norrath.  Many have come from far and wide to be trained in their skills.

**You say:** `warrior guild`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** The warriors of Kaladim are the Stormguard.  The fiercest warriors on all Norrath.  Stormguard Hall is in South Kaladim and includes the white marble arena.

**You say:** `supplies`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** If you are looking for supplies, I can suggest Gurtha's Wares near the bridge in South Kaladim.

**You say:** `bank`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** You can find Ratsbone Treasury next to the mine entrance in North Kaladim.

**You say:** `stormguard`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** In South Kaladim you will find the Stormguard, the warrior guild.  They train on the arena grounds.

**You say:** `rogue guild`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** I have heard rumors of a rogue guild in Kaladim, but have seen none.

**You say:** `healer`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** If you are injured, I suggest you rush to North Kaladim and visit the Underfoot Cathedral.  Just go through tunnel behind Ratsbone Treasury.

**You say:** `mine`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** The mines of Kaladim can be entered in North Kaladim.  The opening is adjacent to the Ratsbone Treasury.

**You say:** `paladin guild`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** Our Paladins of Underfoot can be found to the rear of the Cathedral of Underfoot in North Kaladim.

**You say:** `cathedral`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** The Cathedral of Underfoot is the home of the clerics and paladins of Kaladim.  It is found in North Kaladim through a tunnel behind Ratsbone Treasury.

**You say:** `kiln`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** I know of one public kiln and pottery wheel and they are near the bridge in South Kaladim.

**You say:** `oven`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** There is a public oven in North Kaladim at Greybloom Farm.

**You say:** `leather`



>**Donnel Ratsbome says:** If you seek leather goods, stop by Tanned Assets in South Kaladim.  It is near the arena.
