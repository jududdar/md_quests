# a priest of Vallon Zek



[a priest of Vallon Zek](/npc/113179) is a level 51 Giant Cleric that spawns in [Kael Drakkel](/zone/113).



## Dialog

**You say:** `hail`



if **Faction** >= Apprehensive then



>**a priest of Vallon Zek says:** You enter the halls of the great Temple of Vallon Zek.  Tread carefully here, little one.


else



**a priest of Vallon Zek says one of the following:**

>I didn't know Slime could speak common. Go back to the sewer before I lose my temper.

>Is that your BREATH, or did something die in here? Now go away!

>I wonder how much I could get for the tongue of a blithering fool? Leave before I decide to find out for myself.

end
