# a Storm Giant Warlord



[a Storm Giant Warlord](/npc/113255) is a level 55 Giant Warrior that spawns in [Kael Drakkel](/zone/113).



## Dialog

**You say:** `hail`



if **Faction** >= Apprehensive then



>**a Storm Giant Warlord says:** The fall of the Coldain and the dragons will some day come.  We giants will stride this land as the chosen of Rallos Zek once more.


else



**a Storm Giant Warlord says one of the following:**

>I didn't know Slime could speak common. Go back to the sewer before I lose my temper.

>Is that your BREATH, or did something die in here? Now go away!

>I wonder how much I could get for the tongue of a blithering fool? Leave before I decide to find out for myself.

end
